About Us

History

Light Church was founded in the early 1990s with a simple mission — to bring the light of Christ to the heart of Kilimani. What began as a small prayer group under a tree has since grown into a thriving community of believers committed to worship, service, and spiritual growth.

Over the years, Light Church has been a spiritual home for thousands, offering support through youth programs, women’s fellowships, Bible studies, and outreach missions. Our commitment to both the Word and the community has seen the church grow in numbers, facilities, and global partnerships.

Today, Light Church continues to shine as a beacon of faith, hope, and love. We embrace the digital age while staying rooted in the gospel. Whether you're joining us online or in person, you’re part of a family that prays together, grows together, and serves together.

Our Mission

To lead people into a growing relationship with Jesus Christ through worship, discipleship, and community impact.

Our Vision

To be a Christ-centered church that transforms lives, strengthens families, and impacts communities through the power of the gospel."

Our Values
  • Faith – We trust fully in God's Word and His promises.
  • Love – We show unconditional love to all people.
  • Worship – We honor God through passionate, Spirit-led worship.
  • Service – We believe every believer is called to serve.
  • Discipleship – We grow deeper in faith through teaching and mentoring.
  • Community – We actively seek to make a difference in our neighborhood and beyond.

Pastoral Team

At the heart of Light Church is a dedicated pastoral team called to shepherd, teach, and serve. Each member brings unique gifts, experiences, and a deep commitment to guiding our church family in faith. Together, they lead with humility, wisdom, and a passion for seeing lives transformed by the gospel.
